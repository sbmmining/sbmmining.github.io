Garnet Crushing Plant

garnet ore characteristics and properties

Because the chemical composition of garnet varies, the atomic bonds in some species are stronger than in others. As a result, this mineral group shows a range of hardness on the Mohs scale of about 6.5 to 7.5 which is the right hardness our garnet crusher and garnet grinding mill can process. The harder species, like almandine, are often used for abrasive purposes.

garnet crushing and processing

Large-sized garnet is fed from material hopper uniformly into jaw crusher by vibrating screen for coarse crushing. Then the crushed garnet is transported by belt conveyor into impact crusher or cone crusher for secondary crushing.

The fineness garnet also needs be transported via belt conveyor for screening different particle granularity big and small sized garnet. The matched garnet is transported via belt conveyor to finished product pile. The unmatched granularity garnet is transported back into impact crusher or cone crusher for re-crushing, forming a circuit cycling. The finished product granularity can be adjusted by combination and classification. What¡¯s more, garnet dust removing equipment can be equipped additional for environmental protection.
