Raymond mill machine installation and commissioning analytical

Raymond mill installation debugging:

Before Raymond mill installation Preparation:

  • when Raymond mill run site yet installed, should be properly safeguarded, exposed surfaces are coated with anti-rust oil, and avoid the sun and rain, to prevent rusting water body, to establish a maintenance system.
  • plant and infrastructure should be installed according to Raymond mill base map size sufficient height and installation location, Raymond mill basis should be high-standard cement and must be buried before being poured reinforced base and to be buried conduit or cable channel . After pouring the cement foundation, there must be a 15-day warranty.
  • should be equipped with 2 to 3 tons lifting tools for installation and maintenance with Raymond.
  • Raymond mill from the factory to use for longer than six months, the central axis of the host system, transmission, roller device, analysis of oil pools should be cleaned inspection, cleaning After checking the various components respond add enough oil.

Raymond mill installation:

  • the first gear hanging into the pit filled base, pay attention to control a certain height, and then use the upper level meter calibration plane , while the actuator mounted on the surface of the bolted.
  • Raymond mill host installation. Before installation pad in the lower plane of the base and the cement foundation and anchor bolts to connect the contact point between the rubber shock pad (see figure), then use the box-shaped horizontal base flat instrument calibration, calibration points for cross crosshairs four points, while the alignment of two half couplings , its different axes should be less than 0.20 mm, E1E2 not parallel degree not greater than 0.1 mm, the gap E1, E2 shall be kept within 5-8 mm.
  • the location and height of plumbing fixtures shall be installed general plan shall be arbitrarily altered and heightening, the pipe joints should be sealed, without leaks after tightening. Electrical equipment should be accurate and reliable, should be carried out after the test machine the parts to be installed.

Raymond mill debugging:

  • An empty load test machine running at no load test machine before, roller device should hold rolled rope nets prison, avoiding contact with the impact of the roller and grinding ring, then the host air test machine running at least 1 hour; Host should be running smoothly, cabinets oil temperature should not exceed 80 ℃, temperature does not exceed 40 ℃. Analysis of the host machine direction of rotation shown in Figure 4.
  • the blower should load the boot, to be operating normally after the load, and then observe its smooth operation, no abnormal noise and vibration, bearing the maximum temperature should not exceed 70 ℃, the temperature rise should not exceed 35 ℃.
  • load test machine running time of less than eight hours, Raymond after the machine is working properly no abnormal noise, the pipe joints seamless wind phenomenon, the test machine again after each fastener tightening, can be put into normal use.
