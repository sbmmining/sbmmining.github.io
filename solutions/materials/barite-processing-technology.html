Barite Processing technology

Barite Processing technology

Barite powder is generally divided into barite coarse powder processing (0-3MM), fine powder processing (20 mesh -400 mesh), and deep processing of fine powder barite (400 mesh -1250 mesh) and powder processing (1250 mesh - 3250 mesh) four types.

The first step: broken

Barite lump materials by crusher to enter the mill feed fineness (15mm-50mm).

The second step: Milling

After the break of the barite small materials through a hoist is sent to the bin, and then by the vibrating feeder will its uniform quantitative into the grinding chamber for grinding. Fine powder processing general selection of LM vertical roller mill, MTW Milling Machine, MTM Trapezium Grinder; ultrafine powder processing general selection of LUM vertical mill, ultrafine HGM ultrafine mill.

Processing Equipment

  • MTW Milling Machine
  • LM Vertical Grinding Mills
  • LUM vertical roller mill

The third step: Classification

After grinding the material through powder selecting machine grading, unqualified powder by the classifier after the return to the host re grinding.

The fourth step: Powder

Consistent with the fineness of the powder sub with air flow through pipes into the dust collector was separated collection, finished powder from the outlet by the conveying device and sent to the finished material bin, re unification with the powder tank or automatic packaging machine for packaging.
